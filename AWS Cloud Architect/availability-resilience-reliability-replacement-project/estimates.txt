The achievable Recovery Time Objective (RTO) for this Multi-AZ, multi-region database is as follows:
- Minimum RTO for a single AZ outage: under 1 minute
- Minimum RTO for a single region outage: 5 to 10 minutes

The achievable Recovery Point Objective (RPO) for this Multi-AZ, multi-region database is as follows:
- Minimum RPO for a single AZ outage: a few seconds to a minute
- Minimum RPO for a single region outage: 5 to 10 minutes
